About Us

At Rennes Job Service, our mission is to revolutionize the way candidates and companies meet.

Based in Rennes, we specialize in recruitment and professional support, with the goal of helping our clients achieve their ambitions. Our team, composed of passionate and committed students, makes it a point of honor to understand the specific needs of the local market. Thanks to our personalized services, we offer quality solutions adapted to everyone's expectations. Whether you are a candidate looking for new opportunities or a company looking for talent, Rennes Job Service is your partner at every step of the process.
